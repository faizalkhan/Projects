Headmasters Welcome Message

Dear Parents / Guardians,

On behalf of the faculty and staff at The Glenelg Schools of Abu Dhabi I am delighted to welcome you to what we anticipate to be an awesome school year!

The Glenelg school of Abu Dhabi with Campuses in Abu Dhabi ,Ruwais ,Madinat Zayed, and Gayathi Serves a student body of 6000 in grades KG-12 .We offer a personalized education through a standard -based rigorous curriculum aligned with the U.S Common Core Standards- National Standards .Through varied AP offerings , college preparatory courses, and extra curricula student activities we promote the love of learning, inquiry, and pursuit of excellence; affording students a comprehensive educational journey .

We are eager to work with you as we recognize the value of a strong relationship between home and school, a partnership with you will make a great difference in your child’s educational experience. As partners, we share the responsibility for our children’s success to ensure they achieve their highest potential in a global, technologically advanced society, where Arabic and Islamic values are honored and cultural differences are respected.

As a parent, I am well aware of the great responsibilities and challenges we face every day to ensure our children are learning and growing; but I’m also aware of the pride and joy we feel when they do! This success can only be achieved if we work together consistently to educate and inspire our children.

The Glenelg team of highly qualified educators, professionals and support personnel is committed to our mission and vision to become a premier school in the region, graduating highly competent, socially responsible citizens and future leaders.

During the upcoming 2014 -2015 school year, we will continue to build upon and improve our mission to create opportunities, unlock potential and inspire excellence.

The brilliant Glenelg team and I feel privileged to be a part of this school community. We thank you for your support and look forward to a very positive and productive year together.


Sincerely,

Wedad Saada, M.Ed.

Head of School

The Glenelg School of Abu Dhabi
